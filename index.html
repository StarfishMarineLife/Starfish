Roadmap


2021

Starfish Presale

Starfish Presale will start on July 10th of 2021. The initial presale will consist of 100 million tokens at a fixed price.

Pancake Swap Listing

Starfish will be publicly launched on Pancake a month after the presale is concluded.

Developer Tokens Lock

We will be locking our tokens prior to public launch.

Influencer Marketing

Starfish will help to grow the community with influencer marketing.

Supply Burn

Starfish will burn roughly 50% of its total supply.

Expansion to More Exchanges

The token will be sold on more exchanges including Bitmart, Whitebit, Certik, CoinGecko, CoinmarketCap, and etc.

Partnerships

We will be partnering with aquariums, and marine research laboratories around the world to further our understanding of the marine environment.

2022/2023

Starfish Wallet

Starfish Wallet will be available for Android and IOS and will consist of a secure wallet with Web3 integration.

Breaking Grounds on Headquarters

Starfish’s first marine restoration location will be located on the coast of Florida.

Marine Research Tools

The lab will be fully equipped with tools to perform our research.

Hire on Staff

We will be hiring a team of marine biologists, chemists and lab technicians.

BlockChain

Starfish will move to its own blockchain.

Internship Program

This will be open for up and coming Marine Biologists and we will be offering scholarships for talented students and possible full time job positions.
