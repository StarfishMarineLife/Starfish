Launch Plan

Presale

The Presale will begin on august 1st, and will aim for 300,000,000 sold at a fixed price of 0.0004 bnb per token. The bnb will be divided in the following ratio: 60% to the liquidity pool, 15% to marketing, 15% for startup costs to the marine research center, and 10% to the developement of the starfish wallet and blockchain. In the event the presale is concluded early this ratio will remain unchanged.
Coins sold during presale will be sold without a tax fee.

Why have a presale?

Limit whales

A presale ensures that all early buyers will purchase at the same price ensuring that no one aquires an unfair share of the supply.

Better Marketing

A presale will provide capital, that will allow for a larger marketing budget, which will allow the starfish name gain notoriety, as well as bring attention the coral reef crisis.

Jump Start Development

Capital from the presale will help to fast track the release of the starfish offical wallet as well as the move of starfish from bsc smart chain to its own chain while perserving the balance of all wallets

Official Launch

The official launch will pair 60% of the BNB from the presale with 50% of the number of tokens from presale on pancakeswap. This will allow those who purchase at presale to have a return on investment. This liquidity will be permently burned to help insure the success of the project. During the official launch is also when tax on tokens will go into effect. This is also when there will be a large increase in the amount of marketing for the coin as it will be funded in part by the presale.

What's next?

After the offical launch is concluded the community will vote on either slowly adding more tokens into the liquidity pool to make prices more stable, but will cause the price to drop in the short term, or to simply burn the rest of the supply. Either way all remaining undistributed tokens will be burned.
