How to Buy


Step 1: Install Trust Wallet

Step 2: Get Binance Smart Chain (BSC)

Install Trust Wallet is available on IOS and Android and will give the user easy access to the Binance Smart Chain.

Buy BNB using Binance.com or Trust Wallet. If using Binance.com, transfer the BNB to your Trust wallet. Click on the BNB on the Trust Wallet 'Wallet' tab and then click on the 'More' button. Click on 'Swap to Smart Chain' and then enter the amount of wanted BSC into the BNB field and then hit 'Swap'.

Step 3: Setup PancakeSwap

Step 4: Swap BSC to STRF

Go to the PancakeSwap Exchange. Click 'Connect' and use the WalletConnect portal to connect your Trust wallet.

Click on 'Select a currency' in the 'To' field and paste the address, , into the 'Search name or paste address' field and click 'import'. Enter the amount of BNB to be traded in the 'From' field. Go to the exchange settings and set the slippage to 10%. Click 'Swap' and it should be completed.

Step 5: Add STRF to Trust wallet

STRF is a BEP20 coin that needs to be added to your Trust wallet portfolio manually. Click on the settings icon in the top right corner of the 'Wallet' tab. Scroll to the bottom and click 'Add Custom Token'. Set the Network to Smart Chain and paste the address, , into the Contract Address field. Set the Name to Seedling, Symbol to STRF, and the Decimals to 18. Click on 'Save' and Starfish should appear in your wallet.
