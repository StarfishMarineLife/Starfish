How to Buy

Step 1: Install Metamask

Metamask is available on IOS, Android, and PC. This will give the user easy access to the Binance Smart Chain.

Step 2: Add Smart Chain to Metamask

Click on the blockchain dropdown menu and select 'Custom RPC'. Fill in the fields accordingly:
Network Name: Smart Chain
New RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
Chain ID: 56
Currency Symbol: BNB
Block Explorer URL: https://bscscan.com

Step 3: Get BSC

You can buy Binance Smart Chain (BSC) from Binance and send it to your Metamask wallet. Click on 'Buy crypto' and then select Binance Coin (BEP-20) in the currency field. Hit 'Continue' and enter your Binance Smart Chain wallet address from Metamask and then hit 'Continue' again. Complete the payment process and then the BSC should be transferred to your wallet.

Step 4: Buy Starfish (STRF)

Connect your wallet to the presale by clicking 'Connect' and then logging into your Metamask wallet. Select the amount that you want to invest in the 'Investment Amount' field. Click 'Buy' and the transaction should be completed.
